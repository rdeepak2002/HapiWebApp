About

According to the University of Rochester, "[...] if you struggle with stress, depression, or anxiety, keeping a journal can help you gain control of your emotions and improve your mental health." In this app, users can track their life by creating diary entries. These entries will be analyzed by a neural network which, in turn, will show users an analysis of their entries and how they feel about objects mentioned in their entries with an overall analysis of the object and an individual analysis of the object within each entry. This app also recommends activities to strength relationsihps with people mentioned within journal entries. A mobile app is available for iOS and Android which allows users to create journal entries on the go.

Features

Accurate Analysis

The analysis of objects in entries is accurate and correctly reflects the emotions of users to a specific object.

Easy to Use

Entering a journal is as simple as adding a title and entry, then clicking post.

Secure

Passwords are encrypted with the SHA256 encryption algorithm and entries are only available to users that are logged in.

Improves Social Life

This app will give you recommendations on how to strengthen your relationship with people mentioned in journal entries.
